Cupcake Repository

Welcome to my webpage regarding one of my favorite hobbies: baking!

Growing up, I never cared much for cooking or using the oven. Even once I went to college, I steared clear of the kitchen! However, several years ago I had to bring in something for a potluck and ended up making Oreo cupcakes. They were delicious and from then on, I was hooked! Although I occassionally bake other things, I'll always have a soft spot in my heart for cupcakes.

To the right, I've included a selection of cupcakes I've made throughout the years. Hovering over each cupcake will tell you what kind of cupcake it is and clicking will take you to the original recipe I used to make the cupcake.
